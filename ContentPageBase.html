Give to the Brooklyn College Foundation

The Brooklyn College Foundation (BCF) provides over $1.5 million in scholarships each year to attract excellent students and to provide them with access to transformative higher education.

The BCF also administers funds that enable the college to recognize and honor faculty distinction in teaching and scholarship as well as to offer stipends for sabbaticals and travel expenses that allow faculty to pursue their scholarly endeavors. In addition, the foundation supports academic programs by providing funds to augment classroom instruction with special lectures, conferences, workshops and distinguished guest speakers.

Student Support

In FY12, the BCF granted a total of $1,619,233 to 1,082 Brooklyn College students as follows:

  • $1,307,202 in tuition scholarships/awards to 901 students
  • $99,331 in emergency grants to 105 students
  • $19,000 in travel awards to 8 students
  • $90,200 in internship stipends to 22 students
  • $103,500 in research fellowship awards to 46 students

Faculty Support

In FY12, the BCF awarded a total of $341,650 to 42 Brooklyn College faculty members as follows:

  • $309,900 in chairs/professorships and awards to 24 faculty members
  • $25,000 in travel awards to 10 faculty members
  • $6,750 in lectureship stipends to 8 faculty members

How to Make a Gift

You may contribute to the Brooklyn College Foundation in the following ways:

  • Make a secure, one-time online donation using your credit card or debit card.
  • Make a gift over the phone by calling our office between the hours of 9 a.m. and 5 p.m. EST at 718.951.5074.
  • Write a check payable to The Brooklyn College Foundation, Inc. and mail it to:
    Brooklyn College Foundation
    2900 Bedford Avenue
    Brooklyn, NY 11210-2889
  • Print out a credit card payment form, complete it, and mail it along with your gift to the address above.
  • Join the Boylan Society, our monthly giving club, to make automatic monthly donations.
  • To make a gift or stock or wire transfer of funds, call the office between the hours of 9 a.m. and 5 p.m. EST at 718.951.5074.
